Frequently Asked

What is Donuts Pho Bagels?

A family-run New Bern shop on S. Glenburnie Rd — fresh donuts, authentic Vietnamese phở & coffee, hand-rolled bagels, breakfast, melts & deli sandwiches, all in one place.

Do you have a drive-thru and delivery?

Yes — order at the drive-thru or get it delivered. Call or text ahead and we'll have it ready.

Do you serve real Vietnamese coffee?

We do — plus Thai tea, lattes, cold brew, frappes, smoothies and milkshakes.

Do you cater?

Yes. Donuts by the dozen, bagels, breakfast and more — fresh, gourmet, made to order. Call to set it up.
