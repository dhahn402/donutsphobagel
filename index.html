Our Story

Why Donuts, Pho & Bagels?

Because life's too short to pick just one favorite. We grew up loving all three — the sweet comfort of a fresh donut, the soul-warming bowl of pho, and the chewy perfection of a real bagel — so we put them under one roof, in the heart of New Bern.

Everything's made fresh, served with a smile, and priced for everybody. Come as you are, leave a little happier. 💛

Good to Know

Frequently Asked

What is Donuts Pho Bagel?

New Bern's one-stop for craft donuts, authentic Vietnamese pho, hand-rolled bagels, and real coffee — open all day.

Do you serve authentic Vietnamese coffee?

Yes — real phin-brewed Cà Phê Sữa Đá (Vietnamese iced coffee), plus hot, coconut, and egg coffee.

What are your signature items?

The Cà Phê Sữa Đá Donut, the Banh Mi Bagel, and our slow-simmered pho broth.

How do I order?

Call or text to order for pickup, or find us on the major delivery apps.

Where are you located?

New Bern, North Carolina — open daily.
